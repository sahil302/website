Welcome to Gorkha Ghar Sewa

At Gorkha Ghar Sewa, we're dedicated to making your life easier by delivering top-notch home services right to your doorstep. Our mission is simple: to save you time and effort by providing a one-stop solution for all your household needs. Our Story Founded in 2024 by Mr. Sahil Chettri, with the visionary guidance of Mr. Rohan Chettri, Gorkha Ghar Sewa is based in the heart of Kalimpong, Darjeeling. Our goal is to revolutionize the way you manage your daily tasks by offering a wide range of services under one roof. Our Services From car rentals to barber services, household cleaning to cooking, we've got you covered. Our team of professionals is trained to provide exceptional service, so you can relax and enjoy your free time. Our Vision We're committed to expanding our services beyond Kalimpong, with plans to reach other states soon. Our vision is to become the go-to home service provider, trusted by families and individuals across the region.
